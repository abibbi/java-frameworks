Who We Are

BC Skateboard Shop: built by skaters, for skaters.

At BC Skateboard Shop, skateboarding is more than just a sport — it's a culture, a lifestyle, and a community. Founded by lifelong skateboarders, our mission is to provide high quality skate gear designed for riders of every level, from beginners learning their first ollie to seasoned pros pushing the limits of street and park skating.

We specialize in premium skateboard decks, trucks, wheels, bearings, and accessories sourced from trusted manufacturers and tested by real riders. Our goal is to deliver durable, performance-driven products that help skaters ride harder, progress faster, and express their personal style.

Beyond gear, BC Skateboard Shop is dedicated to supporting the skate community. We partner with local skate shops, sponsor emerging riders, and contribute to the growth of skateparks and youth skate programs. Skateboarding thrives when the community grows, and we’re proud to be part of that movement.

Whether you're building your first board or upgrading your setup, BC Skateboard Shop is here to keep you rolling.
